WHAT
IS CALLED ART ?

Art,also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art,visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

  1. Painting: Expression on a surface (canvas, paper) using color, including oil, watercolor, and acrylic techniques.
  2. Sculpture: Three-dimensional art created by carving, modeling, or assembling materials like stone, clay, or metal.
  3. Architecture: The art and science of designing and constructing buildings that are both functional and aesthetic.
  4. Literature: Written works that convey stories, ideas, and emotions, including poetry, novels, and scripts.
  5. Music: A universal language of sound, rhythm, and emotion that spans vocal and instrumental compositions.
  6. Theatre: A performing art that brings narratives to life on stage, combining acting, dialogue, set design, and sometimes dance.
  7. Cinema (Film): The art of moving images, integrating visuals, sound, and narrative to create immersive experiences.
